First international trip from UoH (Tirupati Balaji)

BY Saurabh Shrivastav Published on November 2025

A Sacred Journey to Tirupati Balaji

This trip to Tirupati was not just a tour, it was a spiritual experience for all of us. Standing in front of Lord Venkateswara’s temple filled our hearts with peace, devotion, and gratitude.

We started our journey early in the morning with excitement and energy. The long queues, chants of “Govinda Govinda,” and the divine atmosphere made every moment special.

With friends by my side, every step felt lighter. We shared laughs, memories, and prayers, making this trip unforgettable.

The darshan of Balaji gave us a sense of calm and strength. It reminded us to stay humble, focused, and thankful in life.

Fun, Friendship, and Faith

This trip became more meaningful because we experienced it together. From traveling, waiting in lines, to clicking photos, every moment was filled with joy.

We helped each other, encouraged one another, and stayed united throughout the journey. True friendship grows stronger during such shared experiences.

Even small moments like eating together or resting after a long walk became precious memories.

Tirupati taught us that devotion and friendship together create the most beautiful memories.

The Divine Atmosphere of Tirumala

The surroundings of Tirumala are calm, clean, and full of positive energy. The hills, fresh air, and temple bells create a peaceful environment.

Every corner of the temple reflects culture, tradition, and devotion passed down for centuries.

Watching thousands of devotees from different places come with faith was truly inspiring.

It made us realize that belief connects people beyond language, age, and background.

Memories That Will Stay Forever

This trip will always remain close to our hearts. It was a perfect blend of fun, friendship, and faith.

We returned not just with photos, but with stronger bonds and positive thoughts.

Tirupati gave us more than darshan—it gave us peace, unity, and unforgettable moments.

These memories will guide us and remind us of the beauty of devotion and togetherness.
